Himalayan

Persian Breeds

The Himalayan is part of the Persian Breed group and is known as one of the oldest breeds. They are sweet-natured, gentle, and easygoing cats that get along well with others in a calm household. They share similar physical features with the Persian, such as a luxurious coat, a flat face with big cheeks, and large, round eyes.

Main Info

  • Origin: UK
  • Coat Pattern: Solid point, tortoiseshell, bicolor point, tricolor point, tabby or lynx point, smoke and shaded point
  • Coat Length: Longhair
  • Health Issues:
    • Prone to obesity
    • Polycystic kidney disease (PKD)
    • Tear duct drainage
    • Progressive retinal atrophy
